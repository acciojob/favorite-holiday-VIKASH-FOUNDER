Goa

Goa is mainly known for world-famous beaches. Goa is the hub of a variety of alcohols at low prices. Most of the people think that Goa is full of beaches, but Goa has covered one-third of forests. Indian tourism always arranges colourful carnivals in Goa for youth.


    Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Known for its water sports and beach parties.
  2. Fort Aguada – Offers a beautiful view of the Arabian Sea.
  3. Calangute Beach – The largest and most popular beach in Goa.
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – A breathtaking four-tiered waterfall.
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A UNESCO World Heritage site with rich history.

    Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry – A spicy and flavorful seafood curry.
  • Prawn Balchão – Tangy and delicious prawn pickle-style dish.
  • Bebinca – A layered Goan dessert made with coconut milk.
  • Vindaloo – A famous Goan spicy curry with meat and vinegar.
  • Feni – A local drink made from cashew fruit.
